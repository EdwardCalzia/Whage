FAQ

How does Whage work?

Whage works as a medbot using a decision tree to diagnose people who have specific symptoms.

Does Whage replace my doctor?

No, Whage does not replace your doctor and cannot provide an official diagnosis. Instead, Whage is created to work with and support doctors to form a healthier future for all.

What else can we provide?

While we provide the decision tree free of charge, we can connect you with speciflised doctors to best help your symptoms for low prices.
